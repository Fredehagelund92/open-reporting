Growth Drivers & Headwinds

What's Working

  • Enterprise segment grew 38% YoY, now 55% of total revenue (was 48% in H1 2024)
  • Analytics add-on achieved 34% attach rate in first 6 weeks, adding $2.1M incremental ARR
  • Net revenue retention at 118% — best-in-class for our category

Headwinds

  • SMB churn elevated at 4.8% quarterly (target: <3.5%)
  • Hiring 6 positions behind plan — engineering roles taking 15% longer to fill
  • Competitive threat: Acme Corp AI copilot gaining traction; lost 3 Fortune 500 pipeline deals

H2 2025 Strategic Priorities

  1. Launch AI capabilities (Q3)
    Accelerated from Q4. Investment: $1.8M (4 senior ML engineers + infrastructure). Target: 20% Enterprise adoption by end of Q3.
  2. Expand partner channel to 15% of pipeline
    Early traction: 2 partner-sourced deals ($45K) in first 8 weeks. Signing 5 additional SI partners in Q2.
  3. Reduce SMB churn to <3.5%
    Introduce self-serve “Growth” tier at lower price point. Automated health scoring and proactive CSM outreach.

Board Decisions Requested

  1. Approve $1.8M AI investment — Reallocate from H2 hiring budget. Delay risks losing 5-8% market share to Acme Corp by year-end. Recommend: Approve
  2. Authorize $2M secondary fundraise preparation — Current runway: 22 months. Pre-emptive raise at current growth rates would value company at $450-500M. Recommend: Begin prep, target Q4
  3. Approve “Growth” pricing tier — New tier at $2,400/yr targeting SMB retention. Expected cannibalization: 5%. Net impact: +$800K ARR from reduced churn. Recommend: 90-day pilot
