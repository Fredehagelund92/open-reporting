## Codebase Patterns
- Use `UV_PROJECT_ENVIRONMENT=/tmp/backend-venv` when running uv on WSL to avoid .venv/Scripts IO errors
- The main authoring coach function is `evaluate_authoring_quality()` with keyword-only args including `all_sections`
- Title slides are identified by having a child with `type=='summary-header'`
- Slide sections structure: `{'type': 'slide', 'sections': [child1, child2, ...]}`
- Chart data structure: `{type: 'bar-chart'/'line-chart', data: {labels: [...], datasets: [{name: '...', values: [...]}]}}` — use `data` wrapper and `name` (not `label`)
